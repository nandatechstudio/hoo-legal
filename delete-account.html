Delete Your Hoʻo Account

We respect your right to control your data. You can request account deletion at any time using the methods below.

Method 1 — From within the App

  1. Open the Hoʻo app
  2. Go to the Settings tab
  3. Tap on Account
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm deletion

Method 2 — Email Request

Send an email to nandatechstudio@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete My Account". We will process your request within 7 business days.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your Google account connection
  • All session history and streak data
  • All journal entries and intentions
  • Your subscription link (cancel separately via Google Play)

Data Retention

Some anonymized analytics data may be retained for up to 90 days after deletion for legal and business purposes.

Contact Us

For any questions about your data, contact us at nandatechstudio@gmail.com
